How to use:

  • Make sure your webcam can see your hand clearly with good lighting
  • Bring your thumb and index finger closer together to decrease volume
  • Move your thumb and index finger apart to increase volume
  • Keep other fingers relaxed for best detection
  • Make sure to allow camera permissions when prompted
